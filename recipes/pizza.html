Description

Ingredients

  • Lavash flatbread
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Bake lavash for 2 minutes
  3. Remove lavash from oven; place on baking tray
  4. Spread pizza sauce on lavash
  5. Top the lavash with cheese and pepperoni
  6. Bake pizza for another 15 minutes
